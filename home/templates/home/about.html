ABOUT HAPPY BOX

Modern life is stressful and hectic. With so many competing demands for our time and energy, there’s little left over for us. We come last. Our needs are side-lined, our wants are ignored, we’re too busy to look after ourselves. But when our wellbeing becomes an afterthought, it impacts on our health. We feel frazzled, wrung out, and disconnected and eventually, we crash. The Happy Box is a treat box with substance, designed to counter the pressures we face in modern life.

We are bomarded on a daily basis with Scandi buzzwords such as "Hygge", "Lagom", "Lykke" and even Finland's "Kalsarikännit" meaning to get drunk if your pants!! In reality there is no one size fits all and sometimes all we need is a little hug in a box

Maybe you got sopping wet on your way to work or you're sat at home feeling ill and sorry for yourself, we can help you out. With happy box you can order some self-care straight to your front door on demand.

Maybe a friend or family memeber needs cheeering up or a little boost. Sending a happy box is the best way to bridge miles and send smiles.

There is just so much to celebrate in life, so let's get this party started! Delivery is fast and free.

OUR STORY

The idea for Happy Box was ulimately born while looking at an advert for motor breakdown asssist! I thought to myself wouldn't that be a great service for people's emotional needs. Everybody needs a little boost these days and what better way to get it than a speedy delivery of a gorgeous package brimming with fun and hope. From there the idea simply grew into what it is today.

While there are thousands of wonderful monthly subscription boxes out there sometimes we need a little leg up on demand. We may not want to make a monthly commitment, we simply need a boost on the fly or we need to cheer up a close one. Happy Box has covered all sorts of situations.
