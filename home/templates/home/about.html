ABOUT HAPPY BOX

Modern life is stressful and hectic. With so many competing demands for our time and energy, there’s little left over for us. We come last. Our needs are side-lined, our wants are ignored: we’re too busy to look after ourselves. But when our wellbeing becomes an afterthought, it impacts on our health. We feel frazzled, wrung out, and disconnected. And eventually, we crash. The Happy Box is a treat box with substance, designed to counter the pressures we face in modern life.

We are bomarded on a daily basis with Scandi buzzwords such as "Hygge", "Lagom", "Lykke" and even Finland's "Kalsarikännit" meaning to get drunk if your pants!! In reality there is no one size fits all and sometimes all we need is a little hug in a box

STORY

The idea for Happy Box was ulimately born while looking at an avert for motor breakdown asssist! I thought to myself wouldn't that be a great service for people's emotional needs.

While there are thousands of wonderful monthly subscription boxes out there sometimes we need a little leg up on demand.
